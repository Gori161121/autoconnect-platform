# Vehicle Scenarios

This directory contains realistic vehicle intelligence scenarios used to describe how AutoConnect processes different vehicle conditions.

## Scenarios

### Critical Engine Issue

Represents an ICE vehicle with active engine faults, high engine temperature and low battery voltage.

### EV Battery Degradation

Represents an electric vehicle with reduced battery health, high charging cycles and lower estimated range.

### Hybrid Efficiency Drop

Represents a hybrid vehicle with reduced efficiency, low electric range and poor battery usage.

### Risky Driver Behavior

Represents aggressive driving behavior based on hard braking, rapid acceleration and overspeed events.

## Purpose

These scenarios help demonstrate how the platform handles:

- diagnostics
- vehicle health
- EV battery intelligence
- hybrid efficiency
- driver behavior
- insurance risk
- service recommendations
