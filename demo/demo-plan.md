## Demo Goal

The demo will show how AutoConnect connects customers with service providers through AI-powered matching, workflow automation, and marketplace analytics.

---

## Demo Scenario

A customer submits a service request. The system analyzes the request, matches it with suitable providers, automates the booking workflow, and displays marketplace analytics.

---

## Demo Flow

### Step 1 — Customer Request

The customer submits:

- service category
- location
- time preference
- budget range
- service details

### Step 2 — Provider Matching

The system evaluates providers based on:

- availability
- rating
- service category
- location
- past performance

### Step 3 — AI Recommendation

The AI Matching Engine recommends the best provider options and explains the matching logic.

### Step 4 — Workflow Automation

Automation workflows handle:

- provider notification
- customer confirmation
- booking creation
- status updates
- reminders

### Step 5 — Marketplace Dashboard

The dashboard displays:

- booking status
- provider performance
- customer activity
- marketplace KPIs
- AI-generated insights

---

## Demo Outcome

The demo should show how a traditional service marketplace can become an intelligent, automated, and data-driven platform.
