# System Flow Diagram

## AutoConnect Marketplace Flow

```text
Customer Request
        ↓
Marketplace Platform
        ↓
Provider Network
        ↓
AI Matching Engine
        ↓
Workflow Automation
        ↓
Booking & Service Delivery
        ↓
Analytics & Business Intelligence

Layer Explanation
Customer Request

Customers submit service needs, preferences, location, timing, and budget.

Marketplace Platform

The platform organizes requests, services, providers, and marketplace activity.

Provider Network

Providers are matched based on availability, category, rating, location, and service fit.

AI Matching Engine

AI supports intelligent provider recommendations and marketplace optimization.

Workflow Automation

n8n, Make, APIs, and webhooks automate notifications, bookings, reminders, and operational workflows.

Booking & Service Delivery

The system manages service booking, status tracking, and provider-customer coordination.

Analytics & Business Intelligence

Marketplace performance, customer behavior, provider quality, and operational KPIs are analyzed.
