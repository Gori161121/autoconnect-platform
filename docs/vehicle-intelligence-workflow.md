# Vehicle Intelligence Workflow

## Overview

This workflow describes how AutoConnect transforms raw vehicle telemetry into actionable intelligence.

---

## Stage 1 — Vehicle Registration

Vehicle information is registered:

- VIN
- make
- model
- year
- mileage

---

## Stage 2 — Device Connectivity

Vehicle telemetry is collected through:

- OBD-II devices
- Telematics modules
- Future OEM integrations

---

## Stage 3 — Telemetry Processing

Incoming signals:

- RPM
- engine temperature
- battery voltage
- fault codes
- battery health
- charging cycles
- fuel consumption

---

## Stage 4 — Diagnostics Engine

The diagnostics service:

- interprets fault codes
- classifies severity
- identifies affected systems

Output:

- LOW
- MEDIUM
- HIGH

---

## Stage 5 — Vehicle Health Engine

Vehicle health score is calculated using:

- mileage
- active fault codes
- overdue maintenance

Output:

- health score
- risk level

---

## Stage 6 — Driver Analytics

Driving behavior is evaluated:

- hard braking
- rapid acceleration
- overspeed events

Output:

- driver score
- behavior classification

---

## Stage 7 — Insurance Intelligence

Driver and vehicle risks are combined.

Output:

- insurance risk score
- premium multiplier

---

## Stage 8 — Maintenance Intelligence

The platform predicts:

- oil service requirements
- inspection deadlines
- maintenance urgency

---

## Stage 9 — Vehicle Intelligence Engine

All intelligence modules are aggregated into:

- diagnostics summary
- health summary
- driver profile
- insurance profile
- maintenance recommendations

---

## Stage 10 — Mobile Experience

Results are presented through:

- Android application
- iOS application

---

## End Goal

Convert raw telemetry into actionable vehicle ownership intelligence.
