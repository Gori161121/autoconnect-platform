# Project Overview

AutoConnect is an intelligent marketplace ecosystem designed to connect customers, service providers, automation systems, analytics platforms, and AI-powered decision support.

The platform aims to optimize service discovery, provider management, workflow execution, and operational intelligence.

---

## Main Purpose

The main purpose of AutoConnect is to transform traditional service marketplaces into intelligent, automated, and data-driven ecosystems.

---

## Key Areas

- service marketplace management
- provider onboarding
- customer onboarding
- intelligent service matching
- workflow automation
- customer intelligence
- provider analytics
- marketplace analytics
- operational visibility
- decision support

---

## Target Users

- service providers
- freelancers
- agencies
- customers
- marketplace operators
- operations teams

---

## Long-Term Vision

Create a fully intelligent marketplace platform where AI, automation, analytics, and operational intelligence work together to optimize service delivery and business growth.
